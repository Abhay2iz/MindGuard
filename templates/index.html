🧠 MindGuard

AI Powered Stress Detection System

About the Creator

My name is Abhay Agrahari, a Computer Science student passionate about Artificial Intelligence and technology.

I created MindGuard as a prototype AI system that can analyze text and detect possible stress levels using Machine Learning and Natural Language Processing.

This is currently a basic prototype developed as a learning project to explore how AI can assist in early mental health awareness and provide supportive insights.

In the future, I plan to expand this project by adding more advanced capabilities such as AI agents, smarter conversation support, improved stress analysis models, and a more interactive user experience.

My goal is to continue improving MindGuard into a more intelligent and helpful digital system that can support users in understanding and managing their mental well-being.
